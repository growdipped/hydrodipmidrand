Services

The latest in water transfer printing/hydro graphic technology, that allows amazing patterns to be applied to almost any 3D surface. ALMOST ANY SUBSTRATES OR HARD SURFACE MATERIAL ( glass, wood, metal, fibre glass, all plastics etc…) CAN BE COATED. Any object that can be safely submerged in water can be coated. Below we explain to you all the steps we take.

  • Surface Cleaning

    We clean and degrease the part and make sure it is free of debris.

  • Primer

    We prime the part you want us to dip, if the part is plastic we use CPO or ATV primer, if the part is metal we us etch primer.

  • Base coat

    After the prime coat has dry complete we will start with the base coat. The base coat will be the primary colour of the part you would like to dip.

  • Application of Hydro Print

    We gently place the film ( pattern you selected ) onto the water, then we spray a chemical called activator onto the film that will dissolve the plastic of the film. Wait for 10 second and start to submerge the part into the water, making sure we get the film in all corners.

  • Clear coating

    Lastly we spray on a clear coat, we can either make it a gloss clear coat or a matt clear coat. The choice is yours.
